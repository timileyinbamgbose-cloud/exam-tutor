## Best Practices (How to Get the Most Out of ExamsTutor AI)

### âœ… Do's:

**1. Use It Regularly**
- Aim for 3-4 times per week
- Even 20-30 minutes helps
- Consistency beats cramming

**2. Ask Lots of Questions**
- Don't be shy!
- There's no such thing as a "stupid question"
- Ask until you truly understand

**3. Do Practice Questions**
- Practice makes perfect
- Try questions even if you're not confident
- Learn from mistakes

**4. Review Explanations Carefully**
- Don't just check if you're right or wrong
- Read why the answer is correct
- Understand the concept, don't memorize

**5. Follow Your Learning Plan**
- It's designed specifically for you
- Stick with it even when challenging
- Progress compounds over time

**6. Take Breaks**
- Study in 25-30 minute chunks
- Take 5-minute breaks
- Don't burn out

### âŒ Don'ts:

- They help us help you better
- Not graded, just informative
- The more accurate, the better your plan

## Common Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Is ExamsTutor AI replacing my teacher?**
A: No! Think of it as a supplement. Your teacher is irreplaceable. ExamsTutor is like a study buddy for when your teacher isn't available.

**Q: Can I use it during exams?**
A: Absolutely not! That's cheating. Use ExamsTutor for studying and practice only.

**Q: What if I get a wrong answer from the AI?**
A: It's rare, but possible. Always double-check with your teacher or textbook. You can also report wrong answers (click the flag icon).

**Q: Can I access it from my phone?**
A: Yes! Works on phones, tablets, and computers. Best experience on tablet or computer, but phone works fine.

**Q: Does it work without internet?**
A: Partially. You can download content for offline use. Ask your teacher to show you how.

**Q: Will my parents see my progress?**
A: Only if your teacher shares reports. Your data is private.

**Q: How much time should I spend on ExamsTutor?**
A: We recommend 60-90 minutes per week spread across 3-4 sessions. Quality over quantity!

**Q: Can I use it for all my subjects?**
A: Currently supports Mathematics, English, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology for SS1-SS3.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, or ask your teacher to reset it.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! All data is encrypted and follows Nigerian data protection laws (NDPR).
